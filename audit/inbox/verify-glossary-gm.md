# Glossary verification — g through m

Verification pass on 39 glossary entries with slugs starting with letters g-m, run 2026-05-19.

## Fixes applied (g-m)

- llama: corrected "Llama 3 (April 2024) pushed competitive intelligence into 70B and then 405B" to clarify that the 405B was actually Llama 3.1 (July 2024), not the initial Llama 3 release; also added the missing 33B size to the original February 2023 release listing (was "7B/13B/65B", now "7B/13B/33B/65B"); also clarified Llama 4 release as April 2025.
- llama-guard: corrected "Llama Guard 3 (2024) expanded language coverage to eight languages and added image-input support" — image-input support is in the separately released Llama Guard 3 Vision (November 2024), not the text Llama Guard 3 (July 2024). Now reads accurately.
- groq: corrected "tens of megabytes of SRAM" to "roughly 230 MB of on-die SRAM per chip" — the Groq LPU has approximately 230 MB SRAM, which is hundreds of megabytes, not tens.
- mlx: corrected "Mac Studio with 128 GB can run Llama-3-70B at 20+ tokens per second" — community-reported throughput for a quantized Llama-3-70B on a Mac Studio M2 Ultra is closer to 8-12 tok/sec; revised to "high single digits to low teens of tokens per second" with smaller models at "tens to low hundreds".
- multi-agent: corrected "Cognition's 2024 essay" — the "Don't Build Multi-Agents" essay by Walden Yan at Cognition was published June 2025, not 2024.
