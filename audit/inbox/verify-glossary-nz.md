## Fixes applied (n-z)

- qwen: Frontmatter alias "Tongyi Qwen" was incorrect; changed to "Tongyi Qianwen" (the canonical Chinese long-form). Body said Qwen3 shipped "seven base sizes from 0.6B to 235B"; actual Qwen3 release (April 29, 2025) is six dense (0.6B, 1.7B, 4B, 8B, 14B, 32B) plus two MoE (30B-A3B, 235B-A22B). Also rewrote the size enumeration. Summary updated "0.5B" to "0.6B".
- neocloud: Body said "CoreWeave is the public example, with a 2024 NVIDIA investment". NVIDIA's first disclosed CoreWeave investment was in the April 2023 Series B; the much larger $2B round was January 2026. Rewrote to "NVIDIA investments since its April 2023 Series B".
- on-device: Body said Mac Studio runs Llama-3-70B in FP8 at 15-25 tps. FP8 is not the canonical Apple Silicon path (Q4/Q5 GGUF or MLX 4/8-bit are), and community reports put 70B inference at roughly 8-14 tps on M2 Ultra. Rewrote to "Q4 quantization at roughly 8 to 14 tokens per second".
- redpajama: Body dated RedPajama v2 to 2024. Actual release was October 30, 2023 (per Together AI blog). Also v1 was April 17, 2023; added explicit month. Tightened "30T+" to "30 trillion filtered and deduplicated tokens".
- vllm: Body said "maintained as a community project under the PyTorch Foundation as of 2024". PyTorch Foundation hosted-project announcement was May 2025. Rewrote to "joined the PyTorch Foundation as a hosted project in May 2025".
