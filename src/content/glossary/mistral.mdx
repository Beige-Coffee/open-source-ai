The French <G term="open-weights">open-weight</G> contender. Mistral AI launched in 2023 with the
7B-parameter base model under <G term="apache-2-0">Apache 2.0</G>, followed by `Mixtral` 8x7B
(the first widely-used open <G term="mixture-of-experts">MoE</G>) and <G term="mixtral">Mixtral</G> 8x22B. Later models
(Codestral, Mistral Large, Pixtral) span code, multimodal, and
<G term="frontier">frontier</G>-class workloads with varied license terms.

Mistral's license posture is more permissive than Meta's: most base
weights are <G term="apache-2-0">Apache 2.0</G> with no <G term="mau">MAU</G> cap or <G term="acceptable-use">use restriction</G>. The newer
flagship models have moved toward commercial licensing, narrowing
the open part of the catalog over time.

Full coverage at [/projects/mistral](/projects/mistral).
