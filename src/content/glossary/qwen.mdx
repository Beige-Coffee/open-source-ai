Alibaba's open release line, one of the more prolific <G term="open-weights">open-weight</G>
families in 2024 to 2026. Qwen3 (2025) shipped seven base sizes from
0.6B to 235B parameters, plus reasoning-mode and instruction-tuned
variants, all under <G term="apache-2-0">Apache 2.0</G>. The 235B-A22B <G term="mixture-of-experts">MoE</G> flagship landed near
the top of <G term="open-weights">open-weight</G> benchmarks at release.

The breadth of the catalog is the differentiator: a team can pick a
Qwen of the right size for any deployment from <G term="on-device">on-device</G> through
<G term="frontier">frontier</G>-class, with consistent training data, license, and <G term="tokenizer">tokenizer</G>.
Multilingual coverage is unusually strong, particularly for Chinese.
