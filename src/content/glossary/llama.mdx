The model family that anchored the <G term="open-weights">open-weight</G> ecosystem from the
original 7B/13B/65B release in February 2023 onward. Llama 2 added
chat-tuned variants and shipped under the Community License that is
still in use; Llama 3 (April 2024) pushed competitive intelligence
into 70B and then 405B; Llama 4 (2025) shifted to mixture-of-experts
architecture.

The license is the most-discussed aspect. The 700M `MAU` clause and
the <G term="acceptable-use">acceptable-use</G> addendum exclude Llama from <G term="osaid">OSAID</G>-compliant "open
source AI"; the practical effect on most users is none, but the
governance signal matters.

Full coverage on the project page at [/projects/llama](/projects/llama).
