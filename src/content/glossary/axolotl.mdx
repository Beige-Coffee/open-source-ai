A configuration-first <G term="fine-tuning">fine-tuning</G> framework. A user writes a YAML
file describing the base model, the training data, the parallelism
choices, the optimizer, and the <G term="lora">LoRA</G> configuration; Axolotl reads
the YAML and orchestrates the underlying <G term="hugging-face">Hugging Face</G> Transformers,
<G term="peft">PEFT</G>, <G term="trl">TRL</G>, and <G term="deepspeed">DeepSpeed</G> components into a coherent run. The
abstraction makes <G term="fine-tuning">fine-tuning</G> reproducible and version-controlled
the way training scripts often are not.

Axolotl is widely used by community fine-tuners on a budget. Most of
the "we trained a <G term="llama">Llama</G> variant on this dataset overnight" blog posts
in 2024 to 2026 used Axolotl under the hood. <G term="apache-2-0">Apache 2.0</G> licensed.

Full coverage at [/projects/axolotl](/projects/axolotl).
