A specialized index over `embedding` vectors that answers "find the k
vectors most similar to this query vector" in sublinear time. The
canonical algorithms are HNSW (graph-based, fast and accurate) and IVF
(inverted-file with product <G term="quantization">quantization</G>, memory-efficient).

The choice space stratifies by where the index lives. Embedded-in-the-
process (sqlite-vec, LanceDB, DuckDB-vss) suits <G term="local-first">local-first</G> and small
deployments. Standalone servers (Qdrant, Weaviate, Milvus) suit
production <G term="rag">RAG</G>. Cloud-hosted (Pinecone, MongoDB Atlas Vector Search)
suits teams wanting a managed offering. Postgres extensions (pgvector)
suit teams already on Postgres.

In open-source AI the trend through 2024 to 2026 has been toward
embedded vector indexes (LanceDB, sqlite-vec) for <G term="local-first">local-first</G> and edge
deployments, with the standalone servers consolidating on enterprise
features (multi-tenancy, hybrid search, observability).
