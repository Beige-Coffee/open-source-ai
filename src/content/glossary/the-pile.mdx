EleutherAI's 2020 open <G term="pretraining">pretraining</G> corpus, mixing 22 sources including
<G term="common-crawl">Common Crawl</G>, PubMed Central, ArXiv, GitHub, StackExchange, Wikipedia,
books, and email archives. The Pile was the first widely-used open
<G term="pretraining">pretraining</G> corpus for language models and the substrate for the
GPT-Neo and GPT-J releases.

Some constituent sub-datasets in The Pile have since been removed for
copyright reasons (Books3 in particular). The Pile-CC and the cleaner
subsets remain in use. Newer corpora (<G term="redpajama">RedPajama</G>, <G term="fineweb">FineWeb</G>, Dolma) have
displaced The Pile for new pretraining runs, but the curation choices
and the citation of source-mixture proportions in the original paper
set the template.

Full coverage at [/projects/the-pile](/projects/the-pile).
