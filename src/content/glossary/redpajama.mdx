Together AI's April 2023 attempt to reproduce the <G term="llama">Llama</G> 1 training
corpus as an open release. RedPajama v1 (1.2 trillion tokens) combined
<G term="common-crawl">Common Crawl</G>, C4, Wikipedia, books, ArXiv, GitHub, and StackExchange
slices in the proportions documented in the <G term="llama">Llama</G> 1 paper. RedPajama-
v2 (October 2023) scaled to 30 trillion filtered and deduplicated
tokens with additional quality scoring.

The corpus opened the path for open-recipe <G term="pretraining">pretraining</G>. The data
itself is no longer the leading choice (<G term="fineweb">FineWeb</G> has supplanted it for
new training runs), but RedPajama proved out the feasibility of
community-scale open <G term="pretraining">pretraining</G> data.
