NVIDIA's guardrails framework, sitting between an application and an
LLM. NeMo Guardrails uses Colang, a small DSL for describing
canonical user intents, allowed flows between them, and forbidden or
escalation flows. Inputs and outputs are checked against the rules
before reaching the model or the user, with optional fact-check or
moderation steps in the chain.

The pattern complements model-level training. Where <G term="llama-guard">Llama Guard</G>
classifies content after the fact, NeMo Guardrails enforces
conversation-level structure: keep the chatbot on its assigned
topic, refuse off-topic requests, escalate ambiguous cases to a human
review path. <G term="apache-2-0">Apache 2.0</G> licensed.
