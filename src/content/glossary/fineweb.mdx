The <G term="pretraining">pretraining</G> dataset that displaced <G term="redpajama">RedPajama</G> and <G term="the-pile">The Pile</G> as the
default open-corpus choice in 2024. FineWeb is built from Common
Crawl with extensive quality filtering (language identification,
deduplication, quality classifiers trained on edu-like content), and
the FineWeb-Edu subset further filters for educational content.

The release is permissively licensed and includes the full
preprocessing pipeline (<G term="hugging-face">Hugging Face</G>'s datatrove library) so teams
can reproduce or modify the filtering. OLMo 2, several open-research
models, and many community fine-tunes train on FineWeb derivatives.

Full coverage at [/projects/fineweb](/projects/fineweb).
