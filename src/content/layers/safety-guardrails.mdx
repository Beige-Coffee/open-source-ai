The tools and techniques for constraining what a model or agent is
allowed to do. Constraints apply across the production pipeline: at
training time (<G term="constitutional-ai">Constitutional AI</G>, <G term="rlhf">RLHF</G>, <G term="dpo">DPO</G>), at <G term="inference">inference</G> time (output
classifiers like <G term="llama-guard">Llama Guard</G> and <G term="nemo-guardrails">NeMo Guardrails</G>), and at agent runtime
(sandbox-escape evaluations, capability-token enforcement). The open
ecosystem here is growing but not yet at parity with closed labs'
internal safety stacks, which are proprietary by design and increasingly
capability-evaluation-heavy. AISI Inspect, METR, and Apollo Research are
the most-credible open and semi-open evaluators of dangerous
capabilities. The reverse-lock-in risk at this layer is that closed-lab
safety claims cannot be independently verified.
