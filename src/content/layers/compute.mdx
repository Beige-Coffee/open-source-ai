Where silicon physically lives and how it gets accessed. Three
categories share this layer: self-hosted (you own the hardware),
centralized cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP, plus dedicated AI clouds like
CoreWeave and Lambda), and decentralized marketplaces (Akash, io.net,
Hyperbolic, Bittensor, Gensyn). Each trades sovereignty, scalability,
and reliability against each other. This is the layer where
decentralized-infrastructure narratives from Bitcoin, IPFS, and DePIN
most cleanly map to AI workloads, and it is also where the open
ecosystem is closest to economic parity with hyperscalers for <G term="inference">inference</G>.
Training at scale remains a hyperscaler problem; <G term="decentralized-training">decentralized training</G>
is moving but unproven at <G term="frontier">frontier</G> scale.
