The political and architectural patterns that distinguish "open AI"
from "open AI you can actually own." This meta-layer names the framing
distinction the rest of the site is built on: individual sovereignty
(cypherpunk, Bitcoin-and-Lightning-adjacent, personal ownership of
compute and data) versus state sovereignty (the NVIDIA / Mistral / G42
"Sovereign AI" framing where the actor is a nation-state). It also
covers the primitives that make individual sovereignty viable:
decentralized training (Prime Intellect, Nous DisTrO, Templar,
Pluralis), decentralized inference (Petals), local-first patterns
(llama.cpp, Ollama, MLX, personal-AI appliances), and permissionless
agentic payments (L402, x402). Each primitive in isolation does not
displace the centralized incumbent; combined, they compound into a
complete alternative stack an individual or small organization can run
end-to-end.
