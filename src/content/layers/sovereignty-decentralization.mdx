The political and architectural patterns that distinguish "open AI"
from "open AI you can actually own." This meta-layer names the framing
distinction the rest of the site is built on: individual sovereignty
(cypherpunk, Bitcoin-and-Lightning-adjacent, personal ownership of
compute and data) versus state sovereignty (the NVIDIA / <G term="mistral">Mistral</G> / G42
"Sovereign AI" framing where the actor is a nation-state). It also
covers the primitives that make individual sovereignty viable:
<G term="decentralized-training">decentralized training</G> (Prime Intellect, Nous DisTrO, Templar,
Pluralis), decentralized <G term="inference">inference</G> (<G term="petals">Petals</G>), <G term="local-first">local-first</G> patterns
(<G term="llama-cpp">llama.cpp</G>, <G term="ollama">Ollama</G>, <G term="mlx">MLX</G>, personal-AI appliances), and permissionless
<G term="agentic-payments">agentic payments</G> (<G term="l402">L402</G>, <G term="x402">x402</G>). Each primitive in isolation does not
displace the centralized incumbent; combined, they compound into a
complete alternative stack an individual or small organization can run
end-to-end.
