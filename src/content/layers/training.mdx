The tools that take open data plus an architecture and produce weights.
Two related ecosystems share this layer: pretraining infrastructure
(Megatron-LM, DeepSpeed, FSDP, JAX-based stacks) used by frontier labs
to build base models from scratch, and fine-tuning infrastructure
(Unsloth, Axolotl, LLaMA-Factory, HuggingFace TRL) used by individuals
and small teams to adapt existing open-weights models. A third
sub-cluster is decentralized training (Prime Intellect, Nous DisTrO,
Templar, Pluralis), which is still slower than centralized
frontier training but is shipping working models. The single cleanest
sovereignty story of 2026 lives here: fine-tuning Llama-class models
went from "A100 only" to "consumer GPU plus a Mac," and the open
fine-tuning ecosystem is what made that happen.
