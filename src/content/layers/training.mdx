The tools that take open data plus an architecture and produce weights.
Two related ecosystems share this layer: <G term="pretraining">pretraining</G> infrastructure
(<G term="megatron">Megatron-LM</G>, <G term="deepspeed">DeepSpeed</G>, FSDP, JAX-based stacks) used by <G term="frontier">frontier</G> labs
to build base models from scratch, and <G term="fine-tuning">fine-tuning</G> infrastructure
(<G term="unsloth">Unsloth</G>, <G term="axolotl">Axolotl</G>, <G term="llama">LLaMA</G>-Factory, <G term="hugging-face">HuggingFace</G> <G term="trl">TRL</G>) used by individuals
and small teams to adapt existing open-weights models. A third
sub-cluster is <G term="decentralized-training">decentralized training</G> (Prime Intellect, Nous DisTrO,
Templar, Pluralis), which is still slower than centralized
<G term="frontier">frontier</G> training but is shipping working models. The single cleanest
sovereignty story of 2026 lives here: <G term="fine-tuning">fine-tuning</G> <G term="llama">Llama</G>-class models
went from "A100 only" to "consumer <G term="gpu">GPU</G> plus a Mac," and the open
<G term="fine-tuning">fine-tuning</G> ecosystem is what made that happen.
