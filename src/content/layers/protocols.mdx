The wire protocols agents, tools, and models use to talk to each other.
The big three in 2026: MCP (Model Context Protocol, model-to-tool), A2A
(agent-to-agent), and AAIF (agent identification). Plus the agentic
payments rails: L402 (Bitcoin Lightning), x402 (stablecoin), and the
in-development MCP commerce extensions. Protocols matter for sovereignty
disproportionately to their visibility: whoever owns the protocol owns
the integration ecosystem, the way HTTP, SMTP, and BGP did before them.
The specs are notionally open, but governance shapes (foundation
neutrality, multi-vendor consortium vs. seeded-by-one-lab) determine
how durably open they remain.
