How we measure progress. The benchmarks, harnesses, and leaderboards
that decide which models are "better." This is a meta-layer rather than
a stack layer because it observes the rest of the stack rather than
sitting between two production-pipeline layers. The open ecosystem is in
mostly-good shape here: lm-evaluation-harness is the de facto reference
harness, HELM and AISI Inspect are the principled large-scale
frameworks, and LMArena is the most-respected human-preference
evaluation. The unsolved problems are not openness but contamination
(models trained on eval data score artificially well) and saturation
(most public benchmarks no longer distinguish frontier from
near-frontier). HLE, GPQA Diamond, FrontierMath, and SWE-bench Verified
are the current frontier-resistant benchmarks.
